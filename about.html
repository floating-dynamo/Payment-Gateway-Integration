About Us

We are leading the way the world understands, treats and defeats the virus and other life-threatening diseases.

Our mission statement

Our mission is to advance cures, and means of prevention, for catastrophic diseases through research and treatment. Consistent with our vision, no person is denied treatment based on race, religion or a family's ability to pay.
